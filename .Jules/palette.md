## 2024-05-18 - Missing ARIA labels and focus indicators on icon-only buttons
**Learning:** In this application's design system, several icon-only buttons lacked essential accessibility attributes (`aria-label`) and visible focus indicators (`focus-visible` classes). This prevents screen reader users from understanding the button's purpose and hinders keyboard navigation.
**Action:** Always verify that icon-only buttons include descriptive `aria-label` attributes and appropriate `focus-visible` styles for keyboard users.
