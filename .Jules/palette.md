## 2024-05-24 - Added localized ARIA labels to icon-only buttons
**Learning:** In Japanese applications, it's critical to provide localized ARIA labels (e.g., `aria-label="ホームに戻る"`) for icon-only buttons to ensure they are accessible to screen reader users.
**Action:** Always verify that all icon-only interactive elements have proper `aria-label`s, translated into the application's primary language.
